About Restaurantly

Easy Bistro is Erik & Amanda Niel’s continued love affair with food and drink! Classic bistro ideas and Southern ingredients are combined in a new space with an open kitchen and a full bar & raw bar. Since opening in 2005, Easy has evolved continually, and this most recent evolution has culminated in its relocation and re-imagining into a new space in the West Village. At Easy, we're constantly pushing ourselves to find new ways to redefine the dining experience and we are wholeheartedly embracing small shareable plates and large shareable meats, with house-made pasta and all the oysters and raw bar ingredients we can find.
Our mission is to be the most sustainable restaurant in New York by sourcing our ingredients locally, supplementing produce with herbs grown on our rooftop garden, and giving back to the community through urban farming education.
We’d like to invite you to experience the 4 Rivers Smokehouse difference in person. From our friendly staff to our delectable meats, our mouthwatering sides to our decadent desserts, see for yourself what all the fuss is about
